FAQs

What are the benefits of solar energy?

Solar energy reduces electricity bills, lowers carbon footprint, and promotes energy independence. It's a sustainable and renewable energy source.

How do FOXESS products work?

FOXESS inverters convert solar energy into usable electricity, while their batteries store excess energy for later use.

Is solar energy suitable for my home?

Most homes can benefit from solar energy. Factors like roof size, orientation, and location will determine efficiency. Our team can assess your property.

What maintenance do solar systems require?

Solar systems are low maintenance but should be cleaned periodically and checked annually to ensure optimal performance.
